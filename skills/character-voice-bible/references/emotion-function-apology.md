# Emotion Function: Apology

Use this file when the exchange must deal with hurt, responsibility, and whether repair is possible.

## Core Rule

An apology is not complete just because "미안" appeared.

Good apology dialogue usually includes:

- accusation or visible hurt
- initial apology or excuse
- rejection of cheap closure
- more specific admission, cost, or changed behavior

## Common Shape

```text
A: hurt or accusation
B: apology attempt
A: refuses easy closure
B: more specific admission or concrete change
```

## Sample

```text
유나: 미안하단 말로 끝낼 거야?
태경: 그럴 생각 아니었어. 진짜 미안해.
유나: 뭐가 미안한데.
태경: 네가 혼자 버티게 만든 거. 그거부터 잘못했어.
```

## What To Check

- does the apology become more specific over time
- does the hurt speaker demand real cost or proof
- does the exchange move beyond formula
