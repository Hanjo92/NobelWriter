# Emotion Function: Threat

Use this file when danger, leverage, or consequence must shape the exchange.

## Core Rule

Threat works best through certainty and leverage, not shouting alone.

Useful moves:

- state the consequence plainly
- imply knowledge or reach
- narrow the target's options
- let calmness itself become pressure

## Common Shape

```text
A: warning or condition
B: resistance, disbelief, or testing
A: clearer consequence
B: retreat, bravado, or counter-threat
```

## Sample

```text
도식: 지금 그 문 열면 네 이름부터 올라간다.
진우: 겁주면 멈출 줄 알아?
도식: 겁주는 게 아니야. 순서 알려주는 거지.
진우: ...말은 잘하네.
```

## What To Check

- does the threat reduce options
- does the dangerous speaker sound controlled in a way that fits them
- is the consequence legible without over-explaining
