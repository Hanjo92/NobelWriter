# Group Dialogue

Use this file when four or more speakers share a scene.

## Core Rule

Do not give everyone equal weight.

In group scenes, assign functions:

- driver: pushes agenda or conflict
- blocker: resists, delays, or questions
- amplifier: escalates emotion or agreement
- cooler: lowers temperature or restores procedure
- witness: says little but changes what others can say

One character may hold more than one function, but not everyone should do the same job.

## Group Scene Risks

- round-robin dialogue where everyone gets one line for no reason
- all speakers using the same intensity
- identical sentence lengths across the group
- no one controlling topic or tempo
- side characters speaking only to dump exposition

## Sample

```text
팀장: 지금부터는 보고 없이 움직이지 않습니다.
민호: 그럼 이미 놓친 건 어떡합니까.
주희: 따지기 전에 인원부터 다시 세죠.
도윤: 세면 뭐 합니까. 안에 남은 사람이 있는데.
팀장: 그래서 더 질서가 필요하다는 겁니다.
```

## What To Check

- who controls topic
- who breaks order
- who brings procedure back
- who speaks least but changes the room
- whether minor speakers sound like distinct people or generic noise
