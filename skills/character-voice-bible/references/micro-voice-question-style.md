# Micro Voice: Question Style

Use this file when speakers differ in how they ask, test, accuse, or lure information out of others.

## Core Rule

Questions are not neutral. They reveal power and intent.

Common question styles:

- direct demand: "어디 있었어."
- trap question: "그걸 내가 모를 줄 알았어?"
- soft probe: "무슨 일 있었어요?"
- fake option: "지금 말할래, 더 커지고 말할래."
- practical check masking emotion: "연락은 왜 안 됐어."

## Sample Contrast

```text
A: 어디 있었어.
B: 무슨 일 있으셨어요?
```

## What To Check

- who asks short and narrowing questions
- who wraps pressure in politeness
- who avoids real questions and uses statements instead
- whether two speakers test others in the same way

## Drift Warning

Do not make every question explicit. Some speakers pressure by stating conclusions first.
