# Micro Voice: Sentence Length

Use this file when speakers differ through compression, sprawl, or how much they pack into a turn.

## Core Rule

Sentence length should reveal stress handling, not random variety.

Common patterns:

- clipped and decisive
- clipped but hesitant
- medium and controlled
- long and defensive
- long and persuasive

## Sample Contrast

```text
A: 안 돼. 끝.
B: 안 되는 건 아는데, 지금 여기서 멈추면 더 크게 터져.
```

## What To Check

- who shortens under pressure
- who starts adding clauses when cornered
- who can hold a long line without sounding essay-like
- whether line length changes consistently with emotion

## Drift Warning

Do not vary line length mechanically. Tie it to personality and scene pressure.
