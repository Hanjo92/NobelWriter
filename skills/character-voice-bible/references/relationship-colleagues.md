# Colleague Dialogue

Use this file when speakers share work, procedure, and mutual caution.

## Core Rule

Conflict often hides inside task language.

Track:

- who frames risk
- who protects process
- who pushes urgency over procedure
- how much respect remains even during disagreement

## Common Shape

```text
A: procedural concern or objection
B: urgency or practical push
A: narrower risk statement
B: reluctant concession, pushback, or workaround
```

## Sample

```text
수빈: 이 상태로 넘기면 검수에서 걸려요.
태준: 오늘 안 넘기면 일정이 밀립니다.
수빈: 밀리는 것보다 터지는 게 더 커요.
태준: ...좋아요. 대신 수정 범위는 줄입시다.
```

## What To Check

- does work language carry personality instead of flattening it
- do both speakers disagree in distinct ways
- does professionalism remain audible even when the line hardens
