# Family Dialogue

Use this file when familiarity, old memory, and role fatigue shape speech.

## Core Rule

Family members can be informal without being emotionally open.

Track:

- kinship address and omission
- who reuses old grievances
- who assumes obligation without saying it
- who speaks from care through nagging, blame, or tired routine

## Common Shape

```text
A: complaint or demand
B: defensive answer or habitual deflection
A: old grievance or practical pressure
B: retreat, snap, or begrudging action
```

## Sample

```text
누나: 약은 먹었어.
동생: 또 그 얘기야?
누나: 또 안 먹었으니까 그렇지.
동생: ...먹을게. 됐지.
```

## What To Check

- does familiarity sound lived-in rather than generic
- is care hiding inside routine language
- do family members sound too emotionally neat for their history
