# Rival Dialogue

Use this file when both speakers protect face and measure themselves against each other.

## Core Rule

Rivals rarely say the feeling cleanly. They compete through pace, precision, and where they choose to wound.

## Common Shape

```text
A: jab or accusation
B: return jab or cool block
A: sharper claim, often narrowing the target
B: refusal to yield, counterproof, or contempt
```

## Sample

```text
민준: 결국 또 네 식대로 갔네.
서윤: 네가 늦었으니까.
민준: 핑계는 빨라.
서윤: 넌 늘 결과보다 체면이 먼저지.
```

## What To Check

- do the rivals attack in different ways
- does one control pace while the other controls ambiguity
- are they too symmetrical in length, bluntness, or register
