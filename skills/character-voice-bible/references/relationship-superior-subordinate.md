# Superior-Subordinate Dialogue

Use this file when hierarchy and permission shape every turn.

## Core Rule

Status should stay audible even when emotions rise.

Track:

- who may interrupt
- who must justify
- who can issue direct commands
- how far pushback can go before it becomes disrespect

## Common Shape

```text
superior: direction or warning
subordinate: agreement, concern, or controlled objection
superior: pressure or line-setting
subordinate: risk-taking insistence or retreat
```

## Sample

```text
팀장: 지금은 보고부터 올려.
주임: 보고 전에 막아야 할 것 같습니다.
팀장: 선 넘지 마.
주임: 넘는 거 압니다. 그래도 지금 말씀드려야 합니다.
```

## What To Check

- does the subordinate resist through insistence rather than instant casualness
- does the superior control permission, not just volume
- does address stay legible under pressure
