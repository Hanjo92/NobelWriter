# Three-Person Dialogue

Use this file when one extra speaker changes the power map.

## Core Rule

The third speaker must alter the exchange, not just wait for a turn.

Useful third-speaker functions:

- backs one side and raises pressure
- breaks momentum and cools the scene
- introduces missing information
- reframes hierarchy
- forces one speaker to hide what they would have said

## Common Shapes

### Two Against One

```text
A: pressure
B: defense
C: joins A or B and tilts the balance
```

### Hidden Alignment

```text
A: asks
B: answers carefully
C: says something that reveals who they are really with
```

### Interrupt And Re-route

```text
A and B escalate
C cuts in
A or B must change register because C is now present
```

## Sample

```text
서윤: 너 혼자 결정했지.
민규: 시간 없었어.
해준: 시간 없었다고 팀을 빼고 가?
민규: 넌 또 그렇게 몰아가지 마.
서윤: 몰아붙인 게 누군데.
```

## What To Check

- does the third speaker shift alignment or only fill space
- does someone change speech level because the third person entered
- does information move differently after the interruption
