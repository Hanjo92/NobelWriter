# Fusion Fantasy Starter Sets

Use this file when the user wants a chapter 1 start for fusion fantasy.

## Audience Tendency Modifiers

### Male-Oriented

- emphasize strategic advantage, domination risk, and fast escalation through mixed systems
- keep the opening focused on leverage, timing, and power shift

### Female-Oriented

- emphasize status pressure, emotional reversal, family or bond cost, and layered reading of power
- let strategy and relationship tension move together

### Neutral

- balance political intrigue, system contrast, and personal stakes
- keep the mixed-world appeal readable and scene-driven

## Regression Advantage

Core promise:
- the protagonist enters the opening with knowledge that changes the balance immediately

Best protagonist position:
- a returner, regressor, failed noble, or survivor standing at the moment they once lost

Opening patterns:
- the known disaster begins again
- a remembered betrayal repeats with one small difference
- the protagonist cuts a doomed path early because they know the cost

First chapter checklist:
- one remembered threat
- one changed action
- one strategic advantage
- one sign the future may not repeat cleanly

Avoid:
- hiding the regression too long if it is the main hook
- spending the opener on retrospective summary
- making the protagonist omnipotent too early

Micro sample:
```text
종이 울린 건 전생과 똑같은 시각이었다. 다만 이번에는 에단이 고개를 들기 전에 먼저 잉크병을 엎었다. 삼 분 뒤, 제국 감찰관이 문을 열고 들어올 자리에 검은 얼룩이 번져 있었다.
```

Draft block:
```text
Write the first scene of a Korean regression fusion fantasy story.
Goal: Show the protagonist using future knowledge immediately, before the old disaster fully repeats.
Checklist: Include one known threat, one altered action, one gained leverage, and one sign the timeline is not perfectly obedient.
Dialogue rule: Let confidence show through timing and restraint, not long explanation.
Opening rule: Begin at the decisive repeat-point.
```

Revision block:
```text
Revise this chapter opening as Korean regression fusion fantasy.
Goal: Increase strategic clarity and immediate advantage.
Rule: Keep the repeated moment and the changed move sharp. Cut retrospective explanation that slows the opening.
```

## Empire Politics

Core promise:
- personal survival depends on reading power faster than everyone else

Best protagonist position:
- a cadet, heir, secretary, hostage, or technical officer inside a violent hierarchy

Opening patterns:
- appointment, trial, or summons with hidden stakes
- a public order that masks a purge
- a minor administrative detail exposes a major shift

First chapter checklist:
- one hierarchy marker
- one strategic reading the protagonist makes early
- one hidden danger inside procedure
- one next move that is political, not only emotional

Avoid:
- drowning the opener in titles and factions
- making politics feel disconnected from bodily risk
- flattening every voice into exposition

Micro sample:
```text
황궁 인장보다 먼저 도착한 건 탄약 창고 재고표였다. 리나는 그 순서만 보고도 누가 잘못된 쪽에 서 있는지 알아봤다. 쿠데타는 늘 총보다 서류가 먼저 움직였다.
```

Draft block:
```text
Write the first scene of a Korean empire-politics fusion fantasy story.
Goal: Open on one procedural detail that reveals a larger power shift.
Checklist: Include one hierarchy marker, one strategic reading, one hidden danger, and one political next move.
Dialogue rule: Keep speech controlled, layered, and leverage-aware.
Opening rule: Let the political clue arrive before the full faction map.
```

Revision block:
```text
Revise this chapter opening as Korean political fusion fantasy.
Goal: Sharpen leverage and consequence.
Rule: Make the political signal concrete and actionable. Cut any naming load that does not change the immediate risk.
```

## Magic Engineering

Core promise:
- mixed systems produce a practical failure or breakthrough that cannot stay contained

Best protagonist position:
- a junior engineer, artificer, mage-technician, or repair specialist blamed when systems fail

Opening patterns:
- prototype malfunction
- field test breach
- routine maintenance reveals sabotage or impossible efficiency

First chapter checklist:
- one mixed-system device or process
- one failure or unexpected success
- one practical cost
- one reason the incident must be handled now, not reported later

Avoid:
- turning the opener into a manual
- explaining every system component at once
- forgetting human consequence

Micro sample:
```text
마력 전도관은 원래 울지 않는다. 그런데 점검실 바닥에 무릎을 댄 서하는 금속관 안쪽에서 아이 우는 소리 같은 공명을 들었다. 계기판은 정상인데, 출력을 줄이자 오히려 빛이 더 세졌다.
```

Draft block:
```text
Write the first scene of a Korean magic-engineering fusion fantasy story.
Goal: Use one mixed-system malfunction or breakthrough to create immediate practical danger or opportunity.
Checklist: Include one device, one abnormal response, one human cost, and one reason the protagonist must act now.
Dialogue rule: Keep speech technical enough to feel real, but still natural and scene-bound.
Opening rule: Let the malfunction speak before the technical explanation.
```

Revision block:
```text
Revise this chapter opening as Korean magic-engineering fusion fantasy.
Goal: Improve system clarity without losing pace.
Rule: Keep one abnormal machine response at the center and cut any explanation that does not affect the next decision.
```
