# Sample Short Triage

Use this file when you want a very short example for a fast first-pass diagnosis.

## Example

```text
Range checked:
- Chapters 12-16

Complaint:
- The story suddenly feels slower after Chapter 11.

Top issue:
- Severity: High
- Evidence: Chapters 13 and 15 repeat the same refusal beat without adding cost, reversal, or new information.
- First break point: Chapter 13
- Minimal fix: Replace one refusal scene with a consequence scene that forces the protagonist to pay for delaying the decision.

Second issue:
- Severity: Medium
- Evidence: The missing-key setup from Chapter 10 is mentioned again in Chapter 16 but still does not alter action, trust, or stakes.
- First break point: Chapter 16
- Minimal fix: Either cash the setup now with a changed plan or demote it so it stops pretending to be immediate.

Not checked:
- Full arc continuity before Chapter 12
- Character voice drift
- Line-level prose quality
```

## Why This Example Works

- It answers the complaint quickly without pretending to be a full diagnosis.
- It makes scope limits explicit.
- It still identifies first break points and minimal fixes.
